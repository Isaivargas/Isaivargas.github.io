Get Started!

Abstract thinking it´s what you need !

The way that you can achieve an abstract thinking  is breaking down every kind of problem into small little pieces and steps that as a result gives to you the guideline to follow in order to solve any kind of problem. Example:  I wanna know how to make a banana cake.

  • The first step: is to define if you want the simple banna cake or if you are looking for a banna cake with cranberries or some extravagant recipe
  • Second step: Google it and look for the ingredients that you need and compare from different resources if you have all the ingredients that are needed.
  • Third step: check the hole process while you analyze if you have all the tools that are required or incase that there´re one or more  missing tools you need to look for alternatives .
  • Fourth step: get all together and cook the banana cake.
  • Fifth step: verify your results .
